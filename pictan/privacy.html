Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 17, 2026

Pictan ("the App") is operated by corita ("we", "us", or "our"), an independent business based in Japan. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App handles and how. By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

Free users. We do not collect any personal information. All learning progress, settings, and other data are stored locally on your device.

Premium subscribers. When you start a premium subscription, the following information is processed:

  • Sign in with Apple identifier. An anonymized identifier provided by Apple to verify your subscription. Your email address is shared with us only if you choose to share it during sign-in.
  • Purchase information. Information about your subscription status, processed via Apple's In-App Purchase system and RevenueCat (our subscription management provider).

We do not collect names, addresses, phone numbers, payment card details, or any other personal information directly. Payments are handled entirely by Apple.

2. How We Use Information

  • Verify and maintain your premium subscription status across your devices.
  • Respond to support inquiries you send to us.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Apple Inc. Provides the App Store, Sign in with Apple, and In-App Purchase processing. See Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat, Inc. Manages subscription status. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

4. What We Do Not Do

  • We do not track your activity within or outside the App.
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your information.
  • We do not use advertising or analytics SDKs.
  • We do not maintain our own user database for learning data.

5. Data Storage and Retention

Learning progress and settings are stored locally on your device using the operating system's local storage. If you have iCloud Backup enabled, your iOS device may include this data in its backup; that backup is managed by Apple, not by us.

Subscription information is retained by RevenueCat and Apple for the duration of your subscription and as required by applicable law.

6. Your Rights

  • Delete local data. Use the "Reset All Progress" option in the App's settings, or uninstall the App.
  • Cancel subscription. Manage your subscription via Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your iOS device.
  • Request deletion of subscription-linked records. Email support@corita.jp. We will work with our providers to delete records to the extent permitted by law.

7. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently received such information, please contact us so we can address it.

8. International Transfers

Our subscription provider RevenueCat is based in the United States. By using the App's premium subscription, you understand that subscription identifiers may be processed in the United States.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. We will revise the effective date above and, when changes are material, notify users through the App or via email when reasonably possible.
